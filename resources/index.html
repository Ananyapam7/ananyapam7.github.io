Resources

These resources have enriched my studies and I highly recommend them for anyone interested in the respective topics.



Statistics and ML

Machine Learning and Pattern Recognition by CM Bishop

The Bible of Machine Learning

Introduction to Statistical Learning

A comprehensive introduction to statistical methods used in machine learning.

Deep Learning Book by Goodfellow, Bengio, and Courville

In-depth material on deep learning.



Quant Finance

Options, Futures, and Other Derivatives by John C. Hull

A foundational text in derivatives trading.

The Concepts and Practice of Mathematical Finance by Mark S. Joshi

A mathematical approach to financial engineering.

Introduction to Stochastic Calculus by Rajeeva L. Karandikar & B. V. Rao

An introduction to stochastic calculus within the Indian Statistical Institute Series.

Handbook of Financial Time Series

A comprehensive guide to financial time series analysis.

Basic Black-Scholes: Option Pricing and Trading by Timothy Falcon Crack

An explanatory text on Black-Scholes option pricing and trading.

Inside the Black Box: A Simple Guide to Quantitative and High-Frequency Trading by Rishi K. Narang

A simplified exploration into the world of quantitative and high-frequency trading.

Quantitative Primer

A primer on quantitative analysis techniques.



C++

The Cherno's C++ Playlist

An excellent series of tutorials on C++ by The Cherno on YouTube.

Effective Modern C++ by Scott Meyers

Techniques for mastering C++11 and C++14.

CppNuts

A channel which contains very niche C++ interview content



Puzzles and Math

Engel Problems

Problem book by Arthur Engel.

Mathematical Puzzles by Martin Gardner

A delightful collection of mathematical puzzles by the renowned Martin Gardner.

50 Challenging Problems in Probability

Problems that provide a great test of probabilistic thinking.

Mathematical Puzzles by Peter Winkler

Intriguing puzzles that challenge mathematical and logical thinking.

Heard on the Street by Timothy Crack

Quantitative questions from Wall Street job interviews.

Practical Guide to Quantitative Finance Guide by Xinfeng Zhou

A practical guide to quantitative finance interviews.

Websites

A list of websites containing quant puzzles.



Operating Systems

Modern Operating Systems by Mythili Vutukuru

An introduction to operating systems taught at IIT-B.

The Linux Programming Interface

A detailed look at the design and implementation of the Linux kernel.



Data Structures and Algorithms

Dynamic Programming Bootcamp IIT-GN

An 6 video bootcamp on dynamic programming.

DSA One Course

An introductory course on DSA which I found extremely user friendly.

Course on Competitive Programming

Provides an introduction to CP

CSES Problem Set

An essential problemset in CP
